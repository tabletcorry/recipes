## Pie Crust

* 3 c flour
* 1 tsp salt
* 1Â¼ c Crisco
* 1 egg
* 5 T water
* 1 T vinegar

Mix egg, vinegar, and water in small bowl. Set aside.

Mix flour and salt in large bowl. Cut in Crisco. Add liquid ingredients, combine briefly.

Place in plastic bag and refrigerate for minimum 15 minutes.
